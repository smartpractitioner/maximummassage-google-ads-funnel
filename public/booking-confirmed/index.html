Before your visit

  • Arrive about 5 minutes early so you have time to settle in.
  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing. Your therapist will walk you through anything that needs to come off for the session.
  • Just bring yourself. If you have past injuries, current medications, or specific concerns, your therapist will ask about them when you arrive.

What happens next

  • You'll get a confirmation email from Jane (our booking system) with your appointment details and your account login.
  • That email also has a link to your intake form. Please fill it in at least 48 hours before your appointment, so your therapist can read it and plan your session before you arrive.
  • You'll get a reminder before your visit so it doesn't sneak up on you.
  • Need to change or cancel? You can do it anytime from your Jane account, using the link in that email.
  • Questions before your visit? Your therapist will go over everything with you at your intake.
